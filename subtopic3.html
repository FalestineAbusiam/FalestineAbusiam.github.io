Source 1

URL: Phishing Detection: Identifying Phishing Emails and Websites

Site Author: Perception Point

Abstract: This guide by Perception Point provides an in-depth look at phishing detection, emphasizing the importance of identifying phishing attacks early to mitigate threats. It explains the differences between phishing, malware, account takeovers, and business email compromise. The article highlights how machine learning can aid in detecting phishing by recognizing patterns and anomalies in data. It offers practical tips for identifying phishing emails and websites, supported by real-life examples. Additionally, the guide discusses various security tools that can help prevent phishing attacks and outlines how Perception Point's solutions enhance phishing detection.

Source 2

URL: Spear Phishing and Common Cyber Attacks

Site Author: National Counterintelligence and Security Center (NCSC)

Abstract: This document from the NCSC focuses on spear phishing, a targeted form of phishing aimed at specific individuals or groups. It explains how attackers craft counterfeit messages that appear legitimate, often incorporating information relevant to the target to increase credibility. The goal is to trick recipients into revealing sensitive information or granting access to computer systems. The document outlines common tactics used in spear phishing attacks and provides recommendations for individuals to protect themselves, such as being cautious with unsolicited communications and verifying the authenticity of requests.
